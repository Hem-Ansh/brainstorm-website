Features

Everything you need to
go from idea to execution

A full toolkit for creative and knowledge workers who think better with others.

🎯

SmartMatch

Describe your project or what kind of collaborator you need. Our AI parses your intent, scans profiles semantically, and surfaces the most relevant matches — not just keyword hits.

⚡

Brainstorm Sessions

Run AI-facilitated, timed brainstorming sessions with phases — divergent thinking, convergence, and actionable next steps. The AI pushes the room, not just summarizes it.

💬

Honest AI Feedback

Share an idea and get a real critique — not just encouragement. Brainstorm evaluates feasibility, originality, and market potential from multiple angles.

🔒

Private Collab Channels

When two people connect, a private channel is created automatically — just for them. A dedicated space to ideate, share, and build together without noise.
